Hello, I'm Tim Hetland, an English PhD turned product content strategist.

I design with words.

Currently, I’m the Director of Content Strategy at Black Pixel (2017-2018).

Previous roles at Black Pixel (blackpixel.com): Director of Marketing (2015-2017); Digital Content Strategist (2014-2015).

From 2012-2015, I was a co-founder at two technology startups: Showqase and Orto.

During the last few years, I’ve provided content strategy and UX writing on over 20 web and mobile products.

Areas of work include: general IA and UX, user on boarding, alerts/dialogs/errors, nav- igation, settings/menus, empty states, microcopy, landing pages, notifications.

In 2016, I received a PhD in English from Washington State University.

Previously: MA in English (2008, WSU) and BA in English (2006, WSU).

While in academia, I taught English courses and published on the intersections of technology and the humanities.

Courses taught at Washington State University and North Seattle Community College (2006-2014) included expository writing, technical writing, film studies, and literature.

Key publications: a book on teaching the humanities with technology; an article on location-based multimodal, digital storytelling.
